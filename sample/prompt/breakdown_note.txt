You can have access to 4 database:
- `bank_financial_report` : Contain all information of financial report on bank company
- `non_bank_financial_report` : Contain all information of financial report on non bank company
- `company_info`: Contain all information of a company (profile, stock code, industry, ...)
- `sub_and_shareholder`: Contain information about ownership or investment of a company to another company.
